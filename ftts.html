Fire Trace Tubbing Systems

Fire Trace Tubbing Systems offer a unique and highly effective method of fire detection and suppression. These systems use a flexible detection tube that bursts when exposed to heat or flame, instantly releasing fire suppressant directly at the source.

  • Ideal for enclosed and compact spaces
  • Automatic, no power or electronics required
  • Localized suppression reduces collateral damage
  • Cost-effective and easy to install
  • Suitable for electrical panels, vehicles, server rooms, etc.
